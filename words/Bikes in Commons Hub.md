All of the bikes below are tested and deemed road-safe by Alex, on 2026-04-09. I have my notes for each bike below, and a subjective rating for which I would prefer.

As with every bike, before you ride, remember to:
* Adjust seat height till your leg is almost fully extended pressing the pedal down
* Check tire pressure
* Test brakes to get a feel for braking strength
